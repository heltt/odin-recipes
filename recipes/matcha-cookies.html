Matcha Cookies

Learn how to make some delicious matcha cookies!

Ingredients

  • 3/4 cup butter, softened (regular or vegan)
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1 egg
  • 1 Tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups + 2 Tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1 Tablespoon matcha powder, sifted
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 cup semisweet or white chocolate chips or chopped chocolate

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F and line 2 baking sheets with parchment paper.
  2. In a medium bowl, combine flour, sifted match powder, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  3. In the bowl of a standing mixer with the paddle attachment or a handheld mixer, combine butter and sugars, beating until light and fluffy (around 1-2 minutes).
  4. Add in egg and vanilla, then beat until just combined (do not over-mix!).
  5. Slowly add in the dry ingredients and mix until a dough forms. Stir in the chocolate chips.
  6. Use a medium cookie scoop or spoon to make 1.5-2 tablespoon sized balls and place them on the baking sheet.
  7. Bake the cookies (one baking sheet at a time, for even baking) for 8-11 minutes. Remove them from the oven when the edges look set. They will look slightly under-baked in the middle and should not be golden brown!
  8. Remove from the oven and leave them on the baking sheet for 5 minutes, then transfer to a cooling rack to cool completely. Enjoy!
